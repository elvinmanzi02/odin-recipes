Heavenly Harmony: The Ultimate Lasagna Deligh

This lasagna dish is simply divine.Each bite is a harmonious blend of
tender pasta, robust tomato sauce,and melted cheeses. The flavors
are perfectly balanced, with a touch of aromatic herbs adding a
delightful It's a true masterpiece of Italian cuisine, offering
a comforting and satisfying experience that leaves a lasting
impression.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grounded beef
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cook noodles, set aside
  3. Brown meat, add onions, garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, seasoning. Simmer.
  5. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture. Repeat.
  7. Top with sauce, mozzarella, Parmesan.
  8. Bake covered, then uncovered until golden.
  9. Rest. Garnish with basil. Serve hot.
