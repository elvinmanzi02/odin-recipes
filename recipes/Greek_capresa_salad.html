Greek Caprese Salad

Refreshing Greek Caprese Salad: A Mediterranean Delight

Greek Caprese salad is a refreshing combination of tomatoes,
cucumber, red onion, Kalamata olives,and feta cheese. It's
dressed with olive oil, lemon juice, and Mediterranean seasonings,
offering a vibrant and flavorful salad.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Dried oregano

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes into bite-sized pieces and place them in a bowl.
  2. Peel and dice the cucumber into similar-sized pieces and add them to the bowl.
  3. Thinly slice the red onion and add it to the bowl.
  4. Pit the Kalamata olives and cut them in half. Add them to the bowl.
  5. Crumble the feta cheese and sprinkle it over the salad.
  6. Chop the fresh parsley and sprinkle it over the salad as well.
  7. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and lemon juice over the salad.
  8. Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  9. Toss all the ingredients together gently until well combined.
  10. Serve immediately as a refreshing and vibrant salad.
